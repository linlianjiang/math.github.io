The International Conference on Polynomial and Tensor Optimization

Registration

Registration is available online until September 20th, 2018. Please click here to download the form. Please send it to zhougm@xtu.edu.cn, after completing the form. Only if you receive an official confirmation email from the conference secretary, your registration is successful. Invited speakers do not need to make online registration.


Registration Fee

Regular participant: RMB 1000
Student participant: RMB 700

Registration fee will be charged at the time of registering.

The registration fee includes all the meals and a banquet during the conference. The conference meals and the conference banquet cost RMB 700 (USD 100 c.a.) for any accompanying person.

Accommodation

Our conference will be held at Huayin International Hotal, Xiangtan ( 68, Shaoshan East Road, Xiangtan).For the security and convenience concern, we recommend all participants stay in the conference hotel.

Hotel rate:

1. Shared double room (per person per night): RMB 150
2. Standard double room (per night): RMB 280

Contributed Talks

We will accept contributed talks in this conference. The acceptance will be indicated in the confirmation email. The deadline for submitting the title and abstract is October 5th.
