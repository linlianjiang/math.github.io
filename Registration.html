The International Conference on Polynomial and Tensor Optimization,December 17-21, 2018

Registration

Registration is now available online. The deadline is October 20th, 2018. Please click here to download the form. Please send it to zhougm@xtu.edu.cn, after completing the form. Only if you receive an official confirmation email from the conference secretary, your registration is successful.


Registration Fee

Regular participant: RMB 1000
Student participant: RMB 700

Registration fee will be charged at the time of registration at Hua Yin International Hotel.

The registration fee includes all the meals and a banquet during the conference. The conference meals and the conference banquet cost RMB 700 (US$ 100) for any accompanying person.

Contributed Talks

We will accept a few contributted talks. If you are interested in givinig a contributed talk, please mention this in the registration form. The acceptance of a contributed talk will be notified in the confirmation email.Please contact Prof. Guangmin Zhou (zhougm@xtu.edu.cn) about contributed talks. The deadline for submitting the title and abstract is October 20th, 2018.
