The International Conference on Polynomial and Tensor Optimization

International Conference on Polynomial and Tensor Optimization

December 17-21, 2018, Xiangtan, Hunan, China

 

Conference Introduction

The International Conference on Polynomial and Tensor Optimization will be held in the city of Xiangtan, Hunan Province, China, on December 17-21, 2018. The Conference will be organized by School of Mathematics and Computing Science, Xiangtan University. Invited lectures will be given on recent advances of polynomial and tensor optimization, and their applications.
